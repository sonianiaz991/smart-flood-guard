Smart Flood Guard

An AI and IoT based flood early warning system designed to monitor environmental conditions in real time and provide timely flood risk alerts.

What is Smart Flood Guard?

Smart Flood Guard is a complete end-to-end flood monitoring solution that combines hardware sensors, a cloud-connected backend, and a real-time web dashboard. It continuously tracks critical environmental parameters — water level, temperature, and humidity — and alerts users when conditions approach or exceed dangerous thresholds.

The system is built around an ESP32 microcontroller that reads data from a DHT11 sensor and an analog water level sensor. When readings cross predefined thresholds, a red LED and buzzer activate on the hardware, and the dashboard updates with real-time warnings. All data is transmitted to a FastAPI backend and stored in a PostgreSQL database for historical analysis and trend visualization.

How It Works

1. Sense

ESP32 reads water level, temperature, and humidity from hardware sensors every 1.5 seconds. If thresholds are exceeded, the LED and buzzer activate immediately.

2. Transmit

Sensor data is buffered locally and sent in batches via HTTP POST over WiFi to the FastAPI backend, ensuring no readings are lost even under network delays.

3. Visualize

The web dashboard displays live metrics, trend charts, alert history, and risk levels. Data is stored in PostgreSQL for long-term analysis.

Monitored Metrics

Water Level

Analog sensor reading (0–4095) representing current water depth. Primary flood indicator.

Temperature

Ambient temperature in Celsius from the DHT11 sensor. Helps correlate weather conditions with flood risk.

Humidity

Relative humidity percentage from the DHT11 sensor. High humidity often precedes heavy rainfall.

Alert Status

Real-time alert flag based on water level threshold. Activates hardware LED and buzzer when danger is detected.

Why It Matters

Flooding is one of the most destructive and frequently occurring natural disasters worldwide. Early detection and timely alerts can significantly reduce the loss of life and property. Traditional flood monitoring methods are often expensive, slow, and limited in coverage.

Smart Flood Guard addresses this gap by providing an affordable, real-time, IoT-based monitoring solution that can be deployed in flood-prone areas. By combining continuous sensor data with an accessible web dashboard, it empowers communities and decision-makers to act before conditions become critical.

The integration of AI and data analytics allows the system to move beyond simple threshold-based alerts toward predictive flood risk assessment, identifying patterns and trends that may indicate rising danger before traditional methods would detect them.
